Common Questions

How do I rent a bike?
Browse available bikes on the map, tap on a bike to see details, then tap "Rent" to book it.

How do I list my bike for rent?
Create an account, go to your profile, tap "List Bike", add photos and details of your bike.

How do payments work?
All payments are processed securely through Apple Pay. You'll be charged when you confirm a rental.

How do I contact the bike owner/renter?
Once you have an active rental, contact information is shared between both parties.

What if there's an issue with a rental?
Contact us immediately at privacy@wheelifyapp.com and we'll help resolve the issue.
