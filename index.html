WheelifyApp Privacy Policy

Last updated: 07/24/2025

Information We Collect

WheelifyApp collects the following information to provide our bike rental service:

  • Contact Information: Email address and phone number for account creation and authentication
  • Location Data: Precise location to show available bikes near you and enable rentals
  • Photos: Images you upload when listing your bike for rent
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app to improve our service

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for app functionality:

  • User authentication and account management
  • Connecting bike renters with bike owners
  • Processing rentals and payments through Apple Pay
  • Displaying bikes on the map based on your location
  • Enabling communication between users for rentals

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Your information is only shared:

  • With other users as necessary for bike rentals (e.g., your name and contact info when you rent a bike)
  • With Apple for payment processing through Apple Pay
  • With AWS for secure data storage and app functionality

Data Security

Your data is protected using enterprise-grade security through Amazon Web Services (AWS). We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your information.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Access and update your account information in the app
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Contact us with privacy questions or concerns

Children's Privacy

WheelifyApp is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the app or by email.
